cert-manager to issue the webhook serving certificate and inject the CA into the
webhook configurations. cert-manager is a PREREQUISITE — if the install above
succeeded, its CRDs are present. Make sure its pods are Ready, otherwise the
webhook Secret will never be created and the controller pod will stay un-ready:

  kubectl -n cert-manager rollout status deployment/cert-manager-webhook
